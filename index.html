Welcome

Engineering next-generation cardiac and muscle tissue systems for disease modeling, drug testing, and regeneration.

The METS Lab develops biomaterials, biofabrication strategies, and automated functional platforms for rapid and scalable engineering of cardiac and skeletal muscle tissues.

Automated stimulation and imaging platforms for high-throughput tissue analysis and pharmacological screening.

Translation

In situ tissue engineering and microgravity-enabled tissue models for regenerative and translational applications.
