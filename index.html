Miniaturized Cardiac and Muscle Models

We develop biomaterials, biofabrication methods, and automated functional platforms for rapid and scalable engineering of cardiac and skeletal muscle tissues.

In Situ Biofabrication with Light and Sound

We explore minimally invasive strategies to crosslink photo- and sono-sensitive biomaterials directly at tissue defect sites for regenerative applications.

Microgravity Tissue Engineering

We use engineered muscle and cardiac tissues in microgravity and space settings to study accelerated disease-relevant phenotypes and translational applications.
