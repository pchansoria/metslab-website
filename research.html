Research Areas

Engineered Tissue Models

We develop advanced engineered tissue systems to study development, regeneration, and disease in controlled experimental settings.

Functional Tissue Readouts

We integrate structural and functional measurements to understand how engineered tissues behave over time and under perturbation.

Multiscale Biological Integration

Our work connects cellular events to tissue-level organization and system-level behavior to generate deeper biological insight.

Translational Bioengineering

We seek to develop relevant in vitro platforms that can support therapeutic development, screening, and bioengineering innovation.
